Growing up, I would always jump in to solve every tech problem that caught my sight. No matter the size of the problem, I like to deep dive to find out the 'why's & 'how's and come up with plausible solution. This love for problem solving led me to pursue a career in 'technical support'. In my 4 years of experience as a support engineer I have worked on projects of various complexities, worked with clients from different backgrounds but the best part about my job was using data to not only solve these problems but also provide actionable insights. I would like to combine this love for data and problem solving to make a meaningful difference.

I recently graduated with a Masters degree in information systems from University of Washington where I gained hands-on experience solving complex, unstructured business problems while learning executive management principles in strategy, operations, and project management. I am hoping to leverage the skills from my experience and education to further my career in data analytics and program management.

Apart from my professional interests, I have a strong passion for coffee, books, and television series. I am also dedicated to blogging, where I share my personal experiences and offer insights on the books I read.
